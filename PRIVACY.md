Welcome to VIVIVI - Digital AI Girlfriend ("App", "We", "Us", "Our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Account Information
- **Login information:** When you sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, we collect basic information from your account (name, email, user ID)
- **Profile information:** Display name, avatar (if you provide)

### 1.2. App Usage Data
- **Chat history:** Conversations between you and AI characters
- **Character preferences:** Characters you have selected, costumes, backgrounds
- **Game progress:** Level, completed quests, login rewards
- **User preferences:** Sound settings, background music, and other options

### 1.3. Payment Data
- **Transaction information:** In-app purchase history (vcoin, ruby)
- **Payment information:** Processed by Apple App Store and Google Play Store, we do not store your credit card information

### 1.4. Media Data
- **Camera:** We request camera access so you can take photos and videos with VRM characters. This data is only processed on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless you actively save or share it.
- **Microphone:** We use the microphone to record your voice during voice conversations with AI characters. Audio data is processed to convert to text and sent to our AI service.
- **Photo Library:** We request photo library access so you can save and load photos/videos from the app. We only access files you choose to save or load.

### 1.5. Technical Data
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage data:** Usage time, features used, technical errors
- **Device identifiers:** Device ID, advertising ID (if available)
